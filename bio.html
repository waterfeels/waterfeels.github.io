artist bio



Ana Zimitravich is a baker and fledgling artist from Atlanta, GA. Ana sees dough and pastry as a modern ritual rooted in tradition. Taking their fascination for aesthetic and sculpture in dough into their mediums as an artist, Ana utilizes gouache, pen, and pixels to explore themes of truth, mindfulness and voyeurism. As a strong proponent for mental health, they paint their own their own reality as a coping mechanism, juxtaposing what is seemingly peaceful with what is deceptively anxious. Ana is applying to art school to help expound on this infatuation with perspectives of "truth" by exploring their own inner world, and traversing from fine art to digital through interdiscplinary use of code, pixellation, and animation.
